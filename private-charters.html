Your Private Florida Boat Adventure

A private boat charter offers the ultimate in flexibility and exclusivity. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion, planning a group getaway, or simply want complete control over your Florida experience, private charters deliver customized adventures designed around your preferences.

Unlike group tours, private boat charters allow you to set the itinerary, duration, and activities. Want to spend the day snorkeling at multiple reefs? Exploring secluded sandbars? Looking for dolphin encounters or sunset viewing? Your captain and crew will tailor the experience to your exact wishes.

Private charters are ideal for anniversaries, weddings, corporate events, family reunions, bachelor/bachelorette parties, and milestone celebrations. With your own dedicated captain and crew, you'll enjoy personalized service, professional expertise, and the comfort of having the boat entirely to yourself and your group.

Florida's waters offer endless possibilities for private charters. Visit pristine sandbars, explore mangrove ecosystems, discover hidden islands, or simply relax on the deck with loved ones. All while enjoying the freedom to customize every aspect of your journey.

Private Charter FAQs

❯ How many people can fit on a private charter boat?

Boat capacity varies by vessel size. Most private charters accommodate 4-50+ passengers, depending on the boat type. Small sailboats may hold 6-8 people, while larger catamarans can accommodate 30+ people. Ask your operator about capacity and seating options.

❯ Can I customize the itinerary on a private charter?

Absolutely! Private charters are built around your preferences. You can request specific locations, activities, dining options, and duration. Your captain will work with you to create the perfect day on the water while ensuring safety and respecting marine regulations.

❯ What's included in a private charter price?

Typically included: boat rental, captain and crew, safety equipment, and fuel. Some charters include beverages and snacks. Food, alcohol, and special requests may have additional charges. Review your charter agreement for complete details on what's covered.

❯ How far in advance should I book a private charter?

For availability and best pricing, book 2-4 weeks in advance. However, many operators can accommodate last-minute bookings if capacity permits. Popular dates and large groups should book further ahead. Contact your chosen operator for their booking window and availability.
