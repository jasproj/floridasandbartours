Florida Sandbar Tours - FAQ

Everything you need to know before you book.

Pricing

How much do Florida sandbar tours cost? +
Shared group tours start at $40/person (Jupiter pontoon) to $79/person (Destin Crab Island tiki boat). The Glass Bottom Kayak tour in Treasure Island is $62/person. Private Gulf coast charters run $300-$650 for the boat. Key West private sandbar boats are $525-$799.
Is a private sandbar charter worth it in Florida? +
For groups of 4+, the per-person math often works out. A $525 Key West private tiki boat divided by 6 guests = $87.50/person, comparable to shared tours with full flexibility and BYOB privileges.

Best Sandbars

What is the best sandbar in Florida? +
Shell Key Sandbar near Gulfport/St. Pete is the most consistently calm and clear Gulf coast sandbar near Tampa Bay. Crab Island in Destin is the most famous for its social scene. Anclote Key near Tarpon Springs is the least crowded premier sandbar in the Tampa Bay area.
What is Crab Island in Destin Florida? +
A shallow sandbar in Choctawhatchee Bay near Destin, accessible only by boat. Famous for its weekend social scene where dozens of boats anchor together. Depth 1-3 feet. Tiki boat tours run $79-$129/person.
Is Tampa or Clearwater better for sandbar tours? +
Gulfport and Treasure Island (south Pinellas County) are the best launch points - closest to Shell Key Preserve. Clearwater has more water sports operators. Tampa downtown adds transit time to Gulf sandbars.

Timing and Conditions

What time of year is best for Florida Gulf coast sandbar tours? +
November-April: best conditions - cooler, less humid, calm winds, clearer water. Summer (June-September): warm water (85-88F) but daily afternoon thunderstorms. Tours cancel for lightning with refunds.
Can you see dolphins on Florida sandbar tours? +
Yes - extremely common on Gulf coast tours. Tampa Bay, Clearwater Sound, and the Destin backcountry all have active bottlenose dolphin populations. The Shell Key Sunset Cruise specifically includes dolphin watching.

Booking and What to Bring

How far in advance should I book? +
2-3 days for most shared tours. Destin Crab Island needs 1 week ahead in summer/spring break. Private charters need 3-5 days minimum.
Are Florida sandbar tours good for kids? +
Yes - Gulf coast sandbars are ideal. Shallow (1-3 feet), warm, calm water. The Glass Bottom Kayak tour ($62, 3.5 hrs), Shell Key Sunset Cruise ($50), and Jupiter Family Sandbar Cruise ($40) are all family-friendly.
What should I bring? +
Reef-safe sunscreen (no oxybenzone), towel, water shoes, sunglasses, hat, waterproof phone case. BYOB cooler on private charters. Rash guard for longer tours.
What is the Glass Bottom Kayak tour in Treasure Island? +
Guided kayak tour using transparent-bottom kayaks through mangrove tunnels to a local sandbar. 3.5 hours, $62/person. Unique underwater viewing without snorkeling.
