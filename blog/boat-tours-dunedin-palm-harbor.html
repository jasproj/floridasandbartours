Boat Tours from Dunedin and Palm Harbor

Guide • Updated April 2026

Dunedin and Palm Harbor are charming coastal towns on Tampa Bay's northern shore, offering excellent boat tour access to Caladesi Island, dolphin viewing areas, and scenic intracoastal routes. These towns provide less crowded alternatives to Clearwater with quality dining and beach attractions.

Boat Tours from Dunedin

Dunedin is the gateway to Caladesi Island via ferry service. The ferry provides easy access to pristine island beaches and nature trails. Caladesi ferry departs from Honeymoon Island beach near Dunedin. Local operators offer dolphin tours, wildlife cruises, and fishing charters from Dunedin's downtown marina. The town's charming Victorian architecture and local restaurants make pre/post-tour dining excellent.

Palm Harbor Boat Tour Opportunities

Palm Harbor serves as home port for several fishing charter operations and private charter companies. The calm intracoastal waterway near Palm Harbor provides excellent dolphin viewing and scenic routes. Protected marine habitats offer wildlife viewing without requiring long boat rides. Less developed than Clearwater, Palm Harbor offers a quieter boating experience.

**Pro tip:** Bring a [sunglasses](https://amazon.com/dp/B001234567?tag=wandertrav0c1-20) for the best experience.

Exploring Dunedin & Palm Harbor Waterfront

Beyond boat tours, these towns offer beachfront dining, fishing piers, and water sports rentals. Dunedin Beach is family-friendly with gentle waters. The scenic drive between towns provides photo opportunities. Local shops and galleries appeal to those seeking post-tour activities. The quiet atmosphere contrasts with busier beach towns while maintaining excellent tour access.

FAQs

How do I get to the Caladesi ferry from Dunedin?

The ferry operates from Honeymoon Island, about 15 minutes south of Dunedin. Follow signs to Honeymoon Island State Park.

Are there parking fees at Dunedin and Palm Harbor marinas?

Most commercial marinas offer free parking with tour bookings. Check specific operator policies when booking.

**Pro tip:** Bring a [waterproof bag](https://amazon.com/dp/B001234567?tag=wandertrav0c1-20) for the best experience.

What's the difference between Dunedin and Clearwater for boat tours?

Dunedin is quieter and more scenic, closer to Caladesi Island ferry. Clearwater has more operators and variety but is busier.

Can I rent a boat in Dunedin or Palm Harbor?

Yes, several rental companies offer boat rentals by the hour, half-day, or full-day. Advance booking is recommended.
