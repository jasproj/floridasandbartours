Private vs Group Sandbar Tours Florida: Which Should You Book?

Florida's Gulf coast has a broad range of both shared and private sandbar tours at prices that make the math interesting. Here's the actual breakdown with real prices from Gulf coast operators.

Short answer: For solo travelers and couples, shared tours ($40-$79/person) win. For groups of 4+, private charters often match or beat shared per-person costs while giving you full flexibility. A Gulf coast deck boat at $325 for 4 hours split 6 ways is $54/person - less than every shared tour listed.

  • Pace: No schedule pressure for families with kids
