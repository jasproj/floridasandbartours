Florida Family Adventures on the Water

Family boat tours in Florida offer the perfect blend of fun, education, and adventure for visitors of all ages. Whether your kids are toddlers experiencing the ocean for the first time or teenagers seeking exciting activities, Florida's family-friendly boat tours deliver unforgettable experiences.

Our curated selection includes kid-approved tours specifically designed with families in mind. Family Tour FAQs

❯ What age groups are family tours suitable for?

Most family tours accommodate children from age 3 and up, though some accept younger children. Tours are designed for mixed ages and abilities. Check with your tour operator for specific age recommendations and any restrictions based on your child's age and swimming ability.

❯ Are the tours safe for kids who can't swim well?

Yes! All professional family tours provide life jackets and staff trained in water safety. Many focus on shallow water activities where kids can stand or wade. Snorkeling activities include guidance for beginning swimmers. Always inform the crew about your child's swimming ability.

❯ What should I pack for a family boat tour?

Bring sunscreen (SPF 30+), hats, sunglasses, a light cover-up, snacks, plenty of water, and a change of clothes. Include a waterproof bag for valuables. Most operators provide life jackets and snorkeling gear. Check your tour details for what's provided and what to bring.

❯ Will we definitely see dolphins and marine life?

Most family tours have high success rates for wildlife sightings, especially dolphins which are common in Florida waters. However, wildlife encounters are never guaranteed. Many operators offer rain checks if no dolphins are sighted. Tours still provide amazing beach time and water activities.
